## Reflection on Mock Interview Video

We were given days to prepare for the interview before taking the video, and the video required us to be dressed in formal wear. I had it rough because I have social anxiety. This was my first interview, and I didn’t know where to start or what to do.
I tried practicing my interview multiple times so I could remember the questions and the answers without reading them. I don’t know how many times I had to start again because I kept forgetting.
The scariest part was having to remember the questions and answer them without any guidance. But I ended up bravely doing the video and completing the interview. This method of practicing really helped me finish and submit on time.

## Reflection on Coding Markdown CV

We were given a task to create a Markdown portfolio for our CV. It was easy to understand the task at first because we just had to copy our CV content. However, the difficult part was using Markdown, as it was my first time working with it. At that time, I didn’t even understand what a repository was.

I started using AI and YouTube to look for tutorials to help me understand what was going on. It took me a few hours to understand, and at first it was confusing, but I kept trying until I finally understood how it works. I then started applying the basics of Markdown in my CV.

If I didn’t take those steps, I wouldn’t have understood that when writing a heading you use #, and for smaller titles you use *. This experience helped me learn something new, and in the end I was able to complete my work and submit it on time.
